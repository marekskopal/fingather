# FinGather – App Store Description (EN)

FinGather is your ultimate portfolio tracker — manage and optimize all your investments in one place, and take control of your financial future.

Tracking investments across multiple brokers is challenging, and most platforms don't provide the long-term insights that actually matter. FinGather brings everything together: stocks, ETFs, cryptocurrencies, bonds, and mutual funds — all in a single, clear overview.

**KEY FEATURES**

**Real-Time Portfolio Overview**
See the current value, total gain/loss, return, dividend income, FX impact, and fees for your entire portfolio at a glance. Know exactly where you stand at any moment.

**Effortless Broker Integration**
Connect your accounts from Trading212, XTB, DEGIRO, eToro, Interactive Brokers, Binance, Coinbase, Revolut, and more — via API or CSV/Excel import.

**Advanced Analytics**
Dive deep into your performance with detailed breakdowns of realized and unrealized gains, dividend history, tax reports, currency risk, and year-over-year comparisons.

**Benchmark Comparisons**
Measure your portfolio against any benchmark that matters to you — S&P 500, MSCI World, or any individual asset — and see how you really perform.

**Custom Asset Groups**
Organize your investments exactly the way you want. Create custom groups or use built-in categories by country, sector, or industry to understand your allocation at a glance.

**Goals & Strategies**
Set financial goals, track your progress, and define rebalancing strategies to keep your portfolio on target.

**Dividend Tracking & Calendar**
Track all dividend income and see upcoming payments in a calendar view, so you always know when to expect your next payout.

**Price Alerts**
Set price targets for any asset and get notified the moment they are reached.

Whether you're a long-term investor or actively managing your portfolio, FinGather gives you the clarity and tools to make better financial decisions.
